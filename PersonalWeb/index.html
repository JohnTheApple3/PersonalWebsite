About Me

Hello! I'm Macieira, but you can call me João Guimarães. I have a background in environmental and rural tourism, having studied at the Escola Profissional Agrícola Conde São Bento. Additionally, I pursued a degree in tourism at the University of Trás-os-Montes and Alto Douro, although I didn't complete the course.

During my professional journey, I gained valuable knowledge and skills in the field, particularly in areas like teamwork and collaboration. However, my adventurous spirit and desire for new challenges led me to make a bold decision. I decided to leave my job and embark on the Academia de Código FullStack Bootcamp, where I could explore my passion for programming.

This experience has been nothing short of transformative. I've honed my logical thinking and acquired a solid foundation to step into the IT world. My main goal now is to further strengthen my skills, continue learning and growing, and ultimately find fulfillment in the work that I do.

I would describe myself as a fun, curious, and outgoing individual. I thrive on being pushed out of my comfort zone and embracing new opportunities. Whether it's exploring different technologies or taking on complex projects, I'm always eager to tackle challenges head-on.

Join me on this exciting journey as I navigate the world of programming and strive to make a meaningful impact. Let's learn, grow, and find happiness in the ever-evolving realm of IT!
